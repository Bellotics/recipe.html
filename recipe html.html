JOLLOF RICE

Jollof brings everyone together. This recipe has been perfected over time...tested, trusted and loved by many.

WHAT IT TAKES TO PREPAPRE JOLLOF RICE

Prepration time: 30 mins
Cooking time: 1hr 20 mins
Servings: 4

REQUIRED INGREDIENTS TO PREPRARE JOLLOF RICE

  • 2 1/2 cup white rice (I used Basmati)
  • 1 1/2 cup vegetable or chicken stocl
  • 2-4 cubes of maggi cubes
  • 1(12 oz) bag frozen mixed vegetables
  • 2 large yellow onions
  • 1 (28 oz) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (6 oz) can tomato paste
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil (plus a few extra teaspoon for cooking)
  • 1 1-inch fresh ginger
  • 1 habanero pepper
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Start by blending 1 1/2 of your onions, vegetable oil, ginger, and habanero pepper until everything is smooth paste. Transfer to a medium size bowl.

  2. Blend your diced tomatoes until smooth and transfer to the bowl with onion paste. You can mix until well combined.

  3. Dice the remaining onions. Bring a pot to medium-high heat, add your remaining vegetable oil and saute the onions for a few minutes, until it gets fragrant.

  4. Then add the blended tomato/onion mix and cook until it starts cooking.

  5. Add the rice and vegetable stock and cook on high, until it starts cooking.

  6. Bring the heat as low as possible and cover with a lid. Cook for a total of 20-25 mins.

  7. After simmering for about 10 mins, you can add the vegetables. Stir in well, until well combined and cover again, and cook for 15 more mins.

  8. Remove from heat and serve while hot.
